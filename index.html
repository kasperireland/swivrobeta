If you don't have anything to hide from anyone, why should you even care about your online privacy?

Let's not confuse privacy with secrecy; keeping your privacy is different than keeping a secret. Everyone knows what happens on the toilet, but you still choose to close the bathroom door. That's because you want privacy.
