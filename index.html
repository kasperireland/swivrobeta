Features

What really makes Swivro unique?

Complete E2EE

Swivro, currently utilizing XMPP, offers optional end-to-end encryption (E2EE) for messaging. If you don't trust us, simply enable E2EE by clicking the lock icon, and you no longer have to trust anyone.

No Logs

Unlike other communications services such as WhatsApp, we do not log your every move. We truly respect your online privacy, which is why we do not compromise it.

Transparency

We believe in transparency. Swivro tries to be as transparent as possible with its users. If a critical event occurs which compromises or threatens security, we'll let you know.

    ABOUT SWIVRO

    Swivro is an online organization, founded in late 2015, focused on online privacy, cybersecurity, transparency, and free-speech. We are trying to make the internet a more secure and private place to benefit everyone. At this time, we're offering a free-to-use XMPP server featuring optional end-to-end encryption, no unethical or unnecessary logging whatsoever, and complete transparency with our users.
